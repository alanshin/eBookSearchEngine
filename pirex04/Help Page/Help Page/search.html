How to Search

  1. Enter desired query in the "Query Search Box"
  2. Press Enter
  3. Results will be displayed in the "Search Results Window"
  4. Clicking a result from the "Search Results Window" will display its long form in the "Long Form Display Window"
  5. To clear the search, press the "Clear Button"
